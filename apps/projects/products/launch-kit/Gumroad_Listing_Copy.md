## Full Description

**Are you spending hours every week just managing your inbox?**

This workflow was built for real people with real, messy inboxes — multiple accounts, selling platforms, subscriptions, newsletters, and important emails all mixed together.

With this system, you open your AI assistant, paste one prompt, and it does the rest. It reads your emails, groups them by priority, logs everything into your Notion dashboard, and waits for your approval before touching anything.

**What you get:**

- **Core Daily Prompt** — the exact copy-paste prompt that tells your AI how to process your inbox every day. Includes a structured summary format, safety rules (nothing gets deleted without your say-so), and a customisable rules section.
- **Notion Email Dashboard Template** — a ready-to-use Notion page with sections for priority emails, finance alerts, platform notifications, junk review, auto-archive log, sorting rules, and a follow-up tracker.
- **Step-by-Step Setup Guide** — how to connect your email, set up Notion, and run your first session in under 15 minutes.
- **Voice Command Instructions** — how to run the entire workflow hands-free on your phone.
- **Rules Customisation Guide** — how to teach the AI your personal preferences so it gets smarter every time you use it.

**Works with:** Gmail, Outlook, ChatGPT, Manus, Perplexity, and any AI tool that can access your email.

**No coding required. No subscriptions beyond what you already use.**
