## 1. Campaign overview

**Summary:** A two-week, zero-budget organic launch driving first subscribers to the Gnostic mini-courses membership at veilcartography.gumroad.com, using the free Gnostic Gospels Oracle app as the lead magnet and the existing 8-email Module 1 sequence as the conversion engine.

**Primary objective:** First paying subscribers — target **10 subscriptions in 14 days**. (Modest by design: first-launch conversion from a cold-ish audience typically runs 1–3% of engaged traffic; 10 sales also clears Gumroad's ~$100 earnings threshold that unlocks its Emails feature for future campaigns.)

**Secondary objectives:**
- 100+ new MailerLite subscribers via the Oracle app and free Module 1
- Establish a repeatable weekly content rhythm across 2–3 channels that continues after launch

## 2. Target audience

**Primary:** Curious seekers actively deconstructing organized religion (mostly 25–55), searching for what was edited out of the tradition they left. They want direct experience, not doctrine — and they're wary of both dogma and new-age fluff. Your "calm, beginner-friendly" positioning is a genuine differentiator here.

**Secondary:** Established spirituality/occult/esoteric hobbyists who already read Nag Hammadi material and want structured, well-sourced study.

**Where they are:** Reddit (r/Gnostic, r/Gnosticism, r/Deconstruction, r/exchristian), TikTok & Instagram Reels (#gnosticism, #deconstruction, #esoteric), YouTube Shorts, Pinterest (huge spirituality niche, long content shelf-life), Facebook groups.

**Buying stage:** Awareness → consideration. Almost nobody wakes up wanting a "Gnostic mini-course"; they want answers to questions like "why was the Gospel of Thomas excluded?" Lead with the question, not the product.

## 3. Key messages

**Core message:** *Knowledge from direct experience — not belief. Veil Cartography maps what they hid in plain sight, one calm, beginner-friendly mini-course at a time.*

**Supporting messages:**
1. "Gnosis" doesn't mean secret trivia — it means knowledge that lands in the body and changes you. (Proof: Module 1's mistranslation hook — already tested in your email sequence.)
2. These texts were buried for 1,600 years; the Nag Hammadi Library gave them back. You can read them without a gatekeeper. (Proof: direct citations from primary texts.)
3. Beginner-friendly and calm — no robes, no dogma, no £500 gurus. A subscription that costs less than the books it summarizes.
4. Try before you subscribe: ask the free Gnostic Gospels Oracle anything. (Proof: the app itself.)

**Channel tone shifts:** Reddit = cite sources, zero salesmanship. TikTok/Reels = one provocative question per video. Email = the intimate teacher voice already in your Veil Cartography sequence.

## 8. Risks and mitigations

1. **Reddit backlash against self-promotion** — the community is the most valuable and least tolerant. Mitigation: value posts only, product link in profile, mention Gumroad only when asked directly.
2. **Short-form gets views but no clicks** — common in esoteric niches. Mitigation: every video CTA points to the *free* Oracle app, not the paid product; let the email sequence do the selling.
3. **Solo-creator burnout in a 14-day daily-posting sprint** — mitigation: both batching weekends are in the calendar; if slipping, drop Pinterest first, protect email + shorts.
