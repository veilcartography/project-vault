## PRODUCTION NOTES FOR CAPCUT / CANVA

- **Font stack:** Cinzel (headings) + EB Garamond (body) — both free on Google Fonts
- **Colour palette:** Background `#0a0806` · Gold text `#c9a84c` · White body `#e8e0d0`
- **Text animation:** Fade-in with 0.3s delay between lines (CapCut: "Fade" transition)
- **Screen recordings:** Record on desktop browser at 1920×1080, then crop to 9:16 in CapCut (centre on the chat area)
- **Canva alternative:** Use "Instagram Reel" template · dark background · paste text over screen recording clips
- **Export:** 1080×1920 · 30fps · H.264 · no watermark
