MailerLite Setup Guide

Free email platform · Up to 1,000 subscribers free · Perfect for Veil Cartography

Step 1 Create Your Free Account
  1. Go to mailerlite.com and click Sign Up Free
  2. Use your Veil Cartography business email if possible
  3. Verify your email address
  4. Complete the profile setup — use Veil Cartography as your company name
Free tier limits

Free forever up to 1,000 subscribers and 12,000 emails/month. More than enough to get started.

Step 2 Create Your Subscriber Group
  1. Go to Subscribers → Groups
  2. Click Create Group
  3. Name it: Veil Cartography Oracle
  4. Create a second group: Gumroad Buyers (for people who have purchased)
Why two groups?

People in "Gumroad Buyers" can be excluded from the course sales emails — so buyers don't keep receiving pitches for something they've already bought.

Step 3 Create the Automation (Email Sequence)

Go to Automations → Create Automation. Build this flow:

🔵 Trigger: Subscriber joins group "Veil Cartography Oracle"
↓
📧 Email 1: Module 1 PDF delivery (send immediately) — subject: "📖 Your Module 1 PDF is inside..."
↓
⏱️ Delay: 7 days
↓
📧 Email 2: The Divine Spark — subject: "✨ You carry a fragment of divine light..."
↓
⏱️ Delay: 7 days · (repeat pattern for Emails 3–8)
↓
📧 Emails 3–8: Add each email from the sequence file, 7 days apart

Copy each email body from your 02-email-sequence.html file and paste into MailerLite's email editor.

Step 4 Upload Your Module 1 PDF
  1. In Email 1, attach the Module 1 PDF as a file link (MailerLite allows file hosting)
  2. Go to Files in your MailerLite account and upload the PDF
  3. Copy the file URL and insert it as a button/link in Email 1: "Click here to download your PDF →"
Alternative

Keep the PDF delivery on the website (as Manus currently has it) and use Email 1 purely as the welcome + confirmation. Either approach works.

Step 5 Get Your API Key for Manus
  1. Go to Integrations → API in your MailerLite account
  2. Click Generate New Token
  3. Name it: Veil Cartography Site
  4. Copy the API key — you'll need it for the Manus integration prompt in your instructions file
Security note

Keep this API key private — don't share it publicly or post it anywhere. Only give it to Manus in a private conversation.

Step 6 Test Everything
  1. Go to your Oracle site and ask 3 questions
  2. Enter your own email address when the gate appears
  3. Check your inbox — Email 1 should arrive within 1 minute
  4. Verify the PDF link works
  5. Check your MailerLite subscriber list — you should appear in "Veil Cartography Oracle" group
