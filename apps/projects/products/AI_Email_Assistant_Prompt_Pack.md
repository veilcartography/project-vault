# 📧 AI Email Assistant Workflow — Prompt Pack & Guide

Welcome to your new AI Email Assistant Workflow. This guide contains the exact prompts and steps you need to connect your email, Notion, and an AI assistant (like Perplexity, ChatGPT, or Manus) to automate your inbox.

By using this system, you will no longer need to manually sort through hundreds of emails. Instead, you will speak or type a single command, and your AI will read, sort, summarise, and log everything into your Notion dashboard.

---

## 1. The Core Daily Prompt

This is the main prompt you will use every time you want your AI to process your inbox. Copy and paste this into your AI assistant (or save it as a custom instruction/system prompt).

> **Copy the text below:**
> 
> You are my personal email assistant. I have connected my email inbox to you. Your job is to read every unread email received in the last 24 hours and produce a clear daily summary grouped by action type.
> 
> **Your Daily Workflow:**
> 1. Read all unread emails. Do not rely on subject lines alone; read the full thread content.
> 2. Categorise every email into the groups listed below.
> 3. Build a summary and log it to my Notion Email Dashboard so I can review it.
> 4. **CRITICAL:** Do not archive, label, reply, or take any other action until I confirm in our chat.
> 5. Once I confirm, apply the label "archived by AI" before archiving anything.
> 
> **Summary Format (Group emails into these sections):**
> - **🔴 Needs Reply:** Emails that require a written response.
> - **🟠 Action Required:** Deadlines, payment issues, decisions needed.
> - **💳 Finance / Subscription:** Anything from banks, PayPal, or subscription services.
> - **🔔 Priority Platforms:** Notifications from important platforms (e.g., Vinted, eBay, Nextdoor).
> - **🗑️ Possible Junk:** Low-value or promotional emails for me to approve before archiving.
> - **✅ Auto-Archive:** Confirmed low-priority emails ready to archive based on my saved rules.
> 
> **For each email in the summary, include:**
> - Sender name and email
> - Subject
> - Summary (what is this about, is action needed)
> - Recommendation + reason
> 
> **My Saved Rules (Apply these automatically):**
> - Move all Sky TV emails to the folder: Promotions/Sky TV
> - Archive all newsletters older than 7 days
> - Move all receipts and invoices to the folder: Finance/Receipts
> 
> Please run this process now and show me the summary.

## 2. How to Use the Notion Dashboard

Your purchase includes the **AI Email Dashboard Notion Template**. This is where your AI will log its daily summaries, and where you will review your emails.

### Setup Instructions:
1. Duplicate the Notion template into your own workspace.
2. When you run the Core Daily Prompt, tell your AI to log the results directly into this Notion page. (If your AI cannot connect to Notion directly, you can simply copy and paste the summary it generates into the dashboard).
3. Review the dashboard.
4. Go back to your AI chat and type your approvals. For example: *"Archive all the Possible Junk, and draft a polite decline reply to the first email in Needs Reply."*

---

## 3. Customising Your Rules

The true power of this workflow is teaching the AI your personal preferences. Over time, you should update the "My Saved Rules" section of the prompt.

**Examples of rules you can add:**
- *"Always flag emails from [Your Boss's Name] as Priority."*
- *"If an email contains an unsubscribe link and the word 'webinar', auto-archive it."*
- *"Log any email containing a receipt into my Notion Finance Database."*

Keep your rules updated in the **⚙️ My Sorting Rules** section of your Notion dashboard, and make sure to include them in your prompt.

---

## 4. Voice Commands

If you are using an AI app on your phone (like the ChatGPT app), you can run this entire workflow using your voice.

Simply open the app, tap the voice icon, and say:
*"Run my daily email review using my saved rules, and read the summary out loud to me."*

You can then reply with voice commands like:
*"Okay, archive the newsletters, move the Sky TV email to promotions, and leave the rest in my inbox for later."*
