## How to Set It Up

**Step 1 — Choose Your AI Tool**

This workflow works with any AI assistant that can access your email. Recommended options:

- **Manus** (best option — has direct Gmail and Outlook connections built in)
- **ChatGPT** with Gmail plugin or Zapier integration
- **Perplexity** via browser with email open in another tab

**Step 2 — Duplicate the Notion Template**

Open the Notion template link included in your purchase. Click "Duplicate" in the top right corner to add it to your own Notion workspace.

**Step 3 — Customise Your Rules**

Open the Core Daily Prompt document. Find the section labelled "My Saved Rules" and replace the example rules with your own. For example:

- Move all emails from Amazon to Finance/Orders
- Archive all LinkedIn notifications immediately
- Always flag emails from my accountant as Priority

**Step 4 — Run Your First Session**

Open your AI assistant, paste the Core Daily Prompt, and press send. Your AI will read your inbox and return a grouped summary. Review it, then type your approvals.

**Step 5 — Refine Over Time**

Each time you give the AI a new instruction (such as "always archive emails from this sender"), add that rule to your Notion Sorting Rules table and to your prompt. The system gets smarter every time you use it.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Does this work with Gmail and Outlook?**
Yes. The prompt and dashboard work with both. The connection method varies slightly depending on which AI tool you use.

**Do I need a paid AI subscription?**
A free ChatGPT account has limited email access. For the best experience, a ChatGPT Plus subscription or a Manus account is recommended.

**Will the AI ever delete my emails without asking?**
No. The Core Daily Prompt includes a strict rule: the AI must never archive, delete, or reply to any email without your explicit confirmation.

**Can I use this with multiple email accounts?**
Yes. Simply list all your email accounts in the prompt and specify which account each rule applies to.
