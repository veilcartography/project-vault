
# 🗺️ MEMORY ENTRY — AI Onboarding Map

## WHAT THIS PROJECT IS
A guided interactive map/form for people who want AI help but cannot clearly define what they need. Turns vague needs like "more time", "less disorganised", or "get more done" into practical AI workflows. Version 2 exists.

## TARGET USERS
- People who want AI but can't define requirements clearly
- People already using basic tech but not strategically (Office, Google Maps, Google Lens, voice notes)
- People with low technical confidence
- People who fear sharing personal info online
- People with brain injury, memory loss, or executive-function challenges
- Older / less technical users
- Low-income users

## CORE PRODUCT PROMISE
Help a person identify:
- What technology they already use
- What problems they actually need solved
- What AI could do for them in practical daily life
- What level of support / privacy / training they need
- Which tools, prompts, automations, reminders, or tutorials should be created
